Rapid Prototyping

About

Project Length: 2-4 weeks for each game.

Overview: Over the course of a semester, 5 games were created for a rapid prototyping class. Each one was made with a new randomly assigned team, targeting a different theme or gameplay.

Before & Beyond- Unity

Glitches in the timeline have caused worlds to collide, can you fight back against these glitches as you swap between control of two different characters—a knight from a fantasy world, and a hacker from the far future—and learn to use their unique abilities in tandem.

Contributions:

  • Ability Swapping and Character System.
  • UI Programming
  • Enemy Health and Death
  • Settings
  • Interactable System

MooFo - Unity

Utilize a high recoil gun to defend the cows against aliens and platform across the level.

Contributions:

  • User Interface
  • Controller Support.
  • Timer System and Collectible Collection.
  • Scene managment and pause system.

The Means - Unity

Play as a powerful lord, who must maintain their power over their subjects while balancing three resources: military, labor and faith.

Contributions:

  • User Interface: Menus (Animated) and resource UI.
  • Scene managment and pause system.
  • 2D Asset Integration.

The Queen's Guard - Unity

Defeat the hero to defend the queen using combos of different attacks. The hero keeps coming back having learned from his past deaths.

Contributions:

  • AI Learning/Input Tracking
  • Weather System
  • Lighting
  • Event Systems

Scout Mafia - Unity

As a girl scout leader, you must expand your cookie empire. Take control of turf to sell more cookies, grow your troop size, and make more money than your opposition.

Contributions:

  • Turf and Scout Functionality
  • Pause and Scene Management
  • Event Systems
  • Monetization Placeholder Systems
  • Success System
  • Turf, Menu and Gameplay UI
